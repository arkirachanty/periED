What We Do


Let’s be real right now, periods can be kind of freaky and even in this advanced day and age with our gay marriage rights and high-tech Japanese toilets, periods are still stigmatized. As an all girl team, we totally feel the struggle of not knowing what to do when you first get your period, and feeling super uncomfortable for a full week even after having your period for years. Here, we’re hoping to help break the stigma around periods, explain to you how badass having your period is, and we want to offer some of the realest advice for girls when it comes to dealing with Mother Nature.
